About Us

A trusted front door for people in need

Established in January 2022, Casual Minds Matter C.I.C. is a Burnley-based community interest company. We provide a low-threshold, accessible first point of contact for people facing mental health difficulties, housing instability, debt, isolation, and the wider pressures of the cost-of-living crisis.

Our approach is simple: meet people where they are, offer practical help without bureaucratic barriers, and support them before their situation becomes a crisis. We combine professional counselling with hands-on housing casework, group support, and community-based recovery — all under one roof at Howe Walk.

What makes us different is how people find us. Our Howe Walk base operates as a casual clothing shop — people can walk in without feeling labelled. There is no clinical waiting room, no stigma attached to visiting. This low-threshold model means we reach people who would never walk into a traditional mental health service.

Mental Health & Wellbeing

Counselling, trauma-informed therapy, Brainspotting, The Rewind Technique, and peer-led group support in a calm, non-clinical environment.

Housing & Homelessness

Housing stability casework, tenancy sustainment, move-on support, and practical help with benefits, debt, identification and referrals.

Community Recovery

Alcohol and substance misuse workshops, peer-led recovery groups, social prescribing partnerships, and community-based engagement.
